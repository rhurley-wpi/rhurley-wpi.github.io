Recent and Current Work

I currently serve as the student representative to ISIAQ, where I organize webinars, student events, and networking programs. I’m also chairing the student engagement committee for the 2025 Healthy Building Asia Autumn School.

In Fall 2024, I worked at Aerodyne Research through the NSF GEOHEALTH INTERN program on wildfire smoke re-emission and VOC analysis using PTR-ToF-MS instrumentation.

In Summer 2024, I worked with San Jose State's Home Ignition Zone Project using field surveys and drone data to evaluate wildfire preparedness across California communities.
