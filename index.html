About

Second year PhD student at WPI advised by Professor Shichao Liu. Member of the Building Occupants Signals and Synthesis Lab. Research interests include natural disasters, human wellbeing, indoor air quality, and wildfire smoke.

During undergraduate studies at Lafayette College, Rachel studied flash flooding and led indoor air quality projects as part of the REU program in 2020. She also conducted Augmented Reality and mask-wearing cognition studies. Currently she’s engaged in community-based research on smoke impacts in buildings and airborne pollutants.

A varsity runner, Rachel has competed at both the DI and DIII level in Cross Country and Track & Field.
